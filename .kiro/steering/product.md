# Product Overview

Moldova Direct is an e-commerce platform specializing in authentic Moldovan food and wine products with delivery to Spain. The platform serves as a single-seller marketplace (not multi-vendor) targeting Spanish customers who want access to premium Moldovan products.

## Key Business Context

- **Target Market**: Spain (primary), with potential expansion to other EU countries
- **Product Focus**: Moldovan food and wine (authentic, premium quality)
- **Business Model**: Direct-to-consumer, single seller
- **Languages**: Spanish (default), English, Romanian, Russian
- **Currency**: EUR only
- **User Model**: Registration required (no guest checkout)
- **Delivery**: Handled by external logistics partner

## Current Development Status

The project is in active development with completed foundation, product catalog, authentication, and shopping cart functionality. Currently working on checkout and payment integration.

## Key Success Metrics

- Mobile-first user experience (majority of traffic expected from mobile)
- Multi-language support for diverse customer base
- Real-time inventory management
- Seamless checkout process with multiple payment options
